# Proposed Features for Questify

## 1. üéÆ Advanced Game Modes
- **Battle Royale Mode**: Players get eliminated after wrong answers. Last player standing wins.
- **Co-op Challenge**: All players work together to beat a score target against the clock.
- **Knockout Rounds**: Bottom 25% eliminated after every 3 questions.
- **Capture the Flag**: Teams compete to "capture" questions by answering fastest.
- **Sudden Death**: One wrong answer and you're out.
- **Time Attack**: Bonus round where every second counts for massive points.

## 2. üèÜ Progression & Rewards
- **Player Profiles**: Save stats across games (accuracy, win rate, best streak).
- **Achievements System**: Unlock badges (Speed Demon, Perfect Game, Comeback King).
- **Season Leaderboards**: Monthly/weekly competitions with persistent rankings.
- **XP & Levels**: Players level up and unlock cosmetics (avatars, colors, effects).
- **Daily Challenges**: Special objectives for bonus rewards.
- **Streak Rewards**: Visual fire/ice effects for answer streaks.

## 3. üé® Customization & Themes
- **Custom Room Themes**: Host picks from Space, Cyberpunk, Retro Arcade, Halloween, Christmas.
- **Music Packs**: Different background music for lobby, thinking, results phases.
- **Avatar Store**: Unlock new emojis, animated avatars, or custom images.
- **Color Schemes**: Players customize their bubble color from expanded palette.
- **Particle Effects**: Winners get unique confetti styles (fireworks, stars, hearts).
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Per-user preference toggle.

## 4. üìä Analytics & Insights
- **Post-Game Stats**: Detailed breakdown (accuracy %, avg answer time, hardest question).
- **Question Analytics**: Host sees which questions stumped players most.
- **Performance Graphs**: Line charts showing player progression through game.
- **Heatmaps**: Visual representation of which answers were most popular.
- **Export Results**: Download game data as CSV/PDF.

## 5. üéØ Question Enhancements
- **Image Questions**: Display images alongside text.
- **Video Clips**: "Watch this 5-second clip, then answer."
- **Audio Questions**: "Name that song" or sound identification.
- **Multi-Select**: Choose all correct answers from options.
- **Rank Order**: Drag answers into correct sequence.
- **Fill in the Blank**: Type short answers with fuzzy matching.
- **Wager System**: Bet points on confidence level before answering.

## 6. üåê Social & Multiplayer
- **Public Rooms**: Join random games with strangers.
- **Friends List**: Invite specific people, track their stats.
- **Spectator Mode**: Watch games live without participating.
- **Voice Chat**: Optional in-game voice for teams.
- **Chat Reactions**: Send emojis during waiting phases.
- **Rematch**: Instant replay with same players.

- **Question Templates**: Pre-made formats (multiple choice, true/false, etc.).

## 8. ‚ö° Power-Ups & Chaos

- **Replay System**: Watch previous games.
- **Highlights**: Auto-generate "best moments" clips.

## 10. üîß Quality of Life
